Dragon Chicken

Spicy, crispy, and coated with a tangy sauce, Dragon Chicken is a popular Indo-Chinese starter. It's made with marinated fried chicken tossed in a fiery blend of sauces, bell peppers, and onions. Perfect for spice lovers looking for a sizzling treat!

Chicken Biryani

Aromatic and flavorful, Chicken Biryani is a timeless Indian rice dish cooked with spices and marinated chicken. Layered with fragrant basmati rice and slow-cooked to perfection. Every bite bursts with royal taste and tradition.

Virgin Mojito

Refreshing and fizzy, Virgin Mojito is a perfect summer cooler made with mint, lime, and soda. A non-alcoholic mocktail that’s light, zesty, and super hydrating.Great for any occasion or simply to beat the heat!

Red Velvet Cake

Soft, moist, and rich, Red Velvet Cake is known for its deep red color and subtle cocoa flavor.Layered with smooth cream cheese frosting, it’s a dessert made to impress. Ideal for birthdays, celebrations, or sweet cravings.
